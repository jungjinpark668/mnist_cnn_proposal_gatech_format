# Submission Checklist

> A complete checklist of all requirements and steps for submitting the Ph.D. Dissertation Proposal in Georgia Tech ECE. Use this to track your progress.

---

## Pre-Proposal Requirements

- [ ] **Advisor Selection** — Submit advisor selection form at https://secure.ece.gatech.edu/gradforms/ (no later than end of 2nd year)
- [ ] **ECE 8022** — Complete the Professional Communication Seminar
- [ ] **PhD Residency Requirement** — Full-time enrollment for at least 2 semesters while classified as a graduate student
- [ ] **Coursework Plan** — Submit electronic coursework plan at https://secure.ece.gatech.edu/gradforms/ showing completed courses and plan for remaining courses (must be approved by Graduate Affairs Office before proposal distribution)

## Proposal Document Requirements

- [ ] **Format** — Written in Georgia Tech Institute Thesis format (templates: http://www.grad.gatech.edu/theses-dissertations-templates)
- [ ] **Page Limit** — Total proposal does not exceed **35 pages** (including figures, tables, references, and appendices)
- [ ] **Literature Survey** — Included as a chapter/sub-chapter; minimum **5 pages**; written entirely by the student with **no outside help**
- [ ] **Disclaimer Statement** — Signed by both student and advisor, inserted before Table of Contents (DocuSign template: https://www.ece.gatech.edu/current-students/graduate/forms)
- [ ] **Abstract** — Begins with "The objective of the proposed research is..."
- [ ] **Proposal Content** — Contains at minimum:
  - [ ] Concise statement of the PhD dissertation topic
  - [ ] Literature Survey (required)
  - [ ] Summary of research completed (preliminary research)
  - [ ] Outline of research to be completed (proposed research)
  - [ ] Clear summary of the contributions
  - [ ] Work remaining and timeline
  - [ ] Facilities needed
  - [ ] References
- [ ] **English Quality** — Written in acceptable, standard English
- [ ] **Figures/Tables** — Presented consistent with professional publication standards; describing text in close proximity

## Committee Formation

- [ ] **Two reading committee members** selected (advisor + student select together)
- [ ] **At least one** reading committee member is ECE faculty
- [ ] **Committee form** submitted online at https://secure.ece.gatech.edu/gradforms/
- [ ] **External members** (if any) — Inform Academic Office for manual addition

## Submission Steps

1. - [ ] **Visit the ECE Graduate Affairs Office** for coursework verification and advising on submission materials
2. - [ ] **Submit committee form online** at https://secure.ece.gatech.edu/gradforms/
3. - [ ] **Email proposal to Academic Office** (daniela@ece.gatech.edu):
   - [ ] PDF of proposal document with DocuSigned disclaimer form inserted before Table of Contents
   - [ ] Email body contains proposal abstract starting with "The objective of the proposed research is..."
4. - [ ] **Wait for distribution** — Academic Office distributes proposal to committee members (you will receive an email with your proposal memo)

## Post-Submission / Oral Exam

- [ ] **Minimum 2 weeks** between submission and oral presentation (for committee review)
- [ ] **Committee chair** schedules the oral exam via ECE Website: https://www.ece.gatech.edu/contact-graduate-office-current-students
- [ ] **Exam announced** minimum 5 working days in advance
- [ ] **Verify posting** — Check ECE and Graduate Studies websites for correct exam posting; contact Academic Office if errors found
- [ ] **Admission to PhD Candidacy DocuSign form** — Submit the day **before** the oral exam (student responsibility). Use Graduate Coordinator name for "School Chair" and "Graduate Coordinator" fields.

## Day of the Oral Exam

- [ ] **Oral presentation** — Open (public) portion, typically 30-45 minutes
- [ ] **Closed Q&A session** — Committee questions following the presentation
- [ ] **Forms completed and signed:**
  - [ ] Admission to PhD Candidacy DocuSign form (signed by committee, student, school chair, graduate coordinator)
  - [ ] PhD Proposal Committee Chair Report (chair's responsibility)
  - [ ] PhD Proposal Individual Evaluation Form (all committee members)

## Post-Exam

- [ ] **If Conditional Pass** — Complete all conditions specified by the committee
- [ ] **If coursework incomplete** — Final coursework plan certified no later than the end of the semester prior to graduation (must show **43 hours of classes**)
- [ ] **All forms submitted** — Proposal process is not complete until all forms are submitted

---

## Key Deadlines and Constraints

| Requirement | Deadline/Constraint |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Advisor Selection Form | By end of 2nd year |
| Proposal Exam | Within **7 terms** of matriculation (not counting summers) |
| Coursework plan approval | Before proposal distribution to committee |
| Committee review period | Minimum **2 weeks** after submission |
| Exam announcement | Minimum **5 working days** in advance |
| Proposal to defense gap | Minimum **6 months** |
| Maximum proposal attempts | **2 attempts** |
| Final coursework certification | End of semester prior to graduation |
| Total coursework hours | **43 hours** |
| Process duration | Typically 3-8+ weeks from submission to exam |

---

> **Reminder:** The 6-month clock between proposal and dissertation defense starts when the student meets all requirements and is declared passed by the committee, even if forms are still being submitted.
